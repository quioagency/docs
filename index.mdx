## How it works

Company Brain follows a simple three-step cycle: ingest your knowledge, keep it in sync, and surface answers wherever your team works.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your sources">
    Link Notion, Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, or upload files directly. Company Brain indexes your content automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask questions">
    Type a question in plain English through the web app, Slack, or the API. Company Brain searches your knowledge base and returns a direct, sourced answer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay current">
    Continuous sync keeps your knowledge base up to date as documents change. No manual re-indexing required.
  </Step>
</Steps>
