If something isn't immediately obvious, the answers below cover the most common questions our users ask. Browse by category or use your browser's find function to jump straight to what you need.

## Getting Started

<Accordion title="What is Company Brain?">
  Company Brain is an AI-powered knowledge management platform that connects to the tools your team already uses — Notion, Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, and more — indexes their content, and lets anyone on your team ask questions in plain English. Instead of hunting through dozens of tabs, you type a question and get an instant, sourced answer drawn directly from your company's own documents.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does it take to set up?">
  Most teams are up and running in **5 to 10 minutes**. Connecting your first source (for example, a Google Drive folder or a Confluence space) takes about two minutes. Company Brain then begins indexing in the background while you continue working. By the time you've added your second source, the first is usually ready to query.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
  Yes. The **Free plan** lets you connect up to **2 sources**, index up to **200 documents**, and ask up to **50 questions per month** — no credit card required. It's a great way to explore Company Brain with a real subset of your team's knowledge before committing to a paid tier. When you're ready to scale, upgrading to Pro or Enterprise takes seconds from **Settings → Billing**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need technical knowledge to use Company Brain?">
  No. Company Brain is built for every role on your team, not just engineers. Connecting a source is a guided OAuth flow — click, authorize, done. Asking questions works exactly like a chat interface. Advanced features like the REST API and webhook integrations are available for technical users, but they're entirely optional. If you can use Slack, you can use Company Brain.
</Accordion>

## Knowledge & Sources

<Accordion title="What file types does Company Brain support?">
  Company Brain can index the following file formats:

  | Format | Extension |
  |---|---|
  | PDF | `.pdf` |
  | Word Document | `.docx` |
  | Plain Text | `.txt` |
  | Markdown | `.md` |
  | HTML | `.html` |
  | CSV | `.csv` |

  Native formats from connected integrations — such as Notion pages, Google Docs, and Confluence articles — are also fully supported and do not require manual export. Support for additional formats (e.g., `.pptx`, `.xlsx`) is on our roadmap.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How often is my knowledge base updated?">
  Company Brain automatically re-syncs every connected source once every **24 hours**. New documents, edits, and deletions in your source tools are reflected in the knowledge base within that window.

  If you need your knowledge base to reflect a change right away, you can trigger a manual sync at any time: go to **Sources**, select the source, and click **Sync Now**. Manual syncs typically complete within a few minutes for smaller sources.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I connect multiple sources?">
  Yes. The number of sources you can connect depends on your plan:

  - **Free** — up to 2 sources
  - **Pro** — up to 20 sources
  - **Enterprise** — unlimited sources

  You can mix and match any combination of supported integrations (Notion, Google Drive, Confluence, Slack, etc.) across those slots.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens when I delete a source?">
  When you remove a source from Company Brain, all indexed content from that source is permanently deleted from your knowledge base. The original documents in your source tool (Google Drive, Notion, etc.) are **never affected** — Company Brain only removes its own index copy. The deletion takes effect immediately, and the AI assistant will no longer reference that content in future answers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Company Brain index private documents?">
  Company Brain only indexes documents that the connected account is authorized to access. If you connect a Google Drive account that has read access to a shared folder, Company Brain indexes that folder. Documents outside that account's permissions are never touched.

  For Confluence and Notion, space- and page-level permissions are respected. Private pages that the connected service account cannot read will not be indexed. You remain in full control of what Company Brain can see by managing permissions in the source tool itself.
</Accordion>

## AI Assistant

<Accordion title="How accurate are the answers?">
  The AI assistant's answers are **grounded directly in your indexed documents**, which makes them highly accurate for questions your knowledge base covers. Every answer includes source citations — the document title and a link back to the original — so you can verify the information instantly.

  Company Brain does not fabricate facts or fill gaps with guesswork. If the answer isn't in your knowledge base, the assistant says so rather than speculating. Accuracy improves as you add more complete, up-to-date sources.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can the assistant answer questions about general knowledge?">
  No. The Company Brain assistant is intentionally scoped to **your organization's knowledge base**. It will not answer questions about general world knowledge, current events, or topics not covered by your connected sources. This design choice keeps answers trustworthy and relevant — you always know the answer came from your own documents, not from a generic language model's training data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens when the brain doesn't know the answer?">
  When no relevant information is found in your knowledge base, the assistant responds with a clear message explaining that it couldn't find an answer. It may also suggest related documents or topics it did find, in case the question was phrased differently from how the content is written.

  For questions that fall outside the knowledge base, you can configure an **escalation path** in **Settings → Assistant** — for example, routing unanswered questions to a Slack channel or a support inbox so a human can follow up.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use Company Brain in Slack?">
  Yes. Once you install the Company Brain Slack app from **Settings → Integrations → Slack**, anyone on your team can ask questions directly in Slack using the slash command:

  ```
  /brain ask What is our parental leave policy?
  ```

  The assistant replies in a thread with a sourced answer. You can also invite `@CompanyBrain` to any channel to enable it to respond when mentioned. The Slack integration is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
</Accordion>

## Security & Privacy

<Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
  Yes. Company Brain applies industry-standard security practices throughout:

  - **Encryption at rest** — all indexed content is encrypted using AES-256.
  - **Encryption in transit** — all data in motion is protected by TLS 1.2 or higher.
  - **SOC 2 Type II certified** — Company Brain has completed an independent SOC 2 Type II audit, confirming our security controls meet rigorous standards.
  - **Single-tenant storage** — your knowledge base is logically isolated from other customers' data.

  You can request a copy of our security overview or our SOC 2 report by emailing security@companybrain.ai.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Company Brain see my documents?">
  Company Brain indexes only the documents the connected service account is authorized to access. Our systems process document content to build a searchable index, but **your documents are never used to train shared AI models** and are never shared with other customers.

  Employees at Company Brain can access indexed content only when explicitly required to resolve a support issue, and only with your consent. You can review our full data processing practices in our [Privacy Policy](https://companybrain.ai/privacy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I delete my data?">
  Yes, you have full control over your data:

  - **Remove a single source** — go to **Sources**, click the source, and select **Remove Source**. All indexed content from that source is deleted immediately.
  - **Delete your entire workspace** — go to **Settings → Workspace → Delete Workspace**. This permanently deletes all sources, indexed content, team members, and account data. This action cannot be undone.

  If you need to verify that your data has been fully deleted, contact support@companybrain.ai and we'll provide written confirmation within 5 business days.
</Accordion>
