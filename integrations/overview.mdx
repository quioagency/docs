Company Brain works best when it's connected to the places where your team's knowledge already lives. Integrations fall into two categories: **Knowledge Source Integrations** pull content from external tools into your Company Brain knowledge base, and **Access Point Integrations** let your team query that knowledge without ever leaving the tools they use day-to-day.

## Integration Types

**Knowledge Source Integrations** connect Company Brain to the platforms where your documents, wikis, and files are stored. Company Brain indexes the content and keeps it up to date automatically so your answers always reflect current information. Available knowledge sources include Notion, Google Drive, Confluence, and direct file upload.

**Access Point Integrations** bring Company Brain's Q&A capabilities into the tools your team uses for communication and collaboration. Instead of switching to a separate interface, your team can ask questions right where conversations are already happening. Slack is currently available as an access point.
