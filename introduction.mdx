Company Brain is an AI-powered knowledge management platform that turns your team's scattered documentation into a single, searchable intelligence layer. Instead of digging through tabs, channels, and folders to find what you need, you ask a question in plain English and get a direct answer — complete with citations pointing back to the exact source documents.

## Who It's For

Company Brain is built for teams that move fast and can't afford to lose time hunting for information. Whether you're onboarding a new hire, resolving a customer issue, or aligning on a product decision, Company Brain surfaces the right knowledge at the right moment — from the tools your team already uses.

## What You Can Do

- **Ask questions in plain English** — query your entire knowledge base conversationally, without learning a search syntax or knowing which tool holds the answer.
- **Connect your existing tools** — integrate with Notion, Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, and more in minutes, with no data migration required.
- **Get sourced, trustworthy answers** — every response includes citations linking directly to the originating documents, so you can verify and share with confidence.
- **Control who sees what** — apply role-based access controls to ensure team members only access knowledge they're permitted to see.
- **Stay current automatically** — continuous sync keeps your knowledge base up to date as your source documents evolve, so answers never go stale.
